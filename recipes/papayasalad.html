Laotian Papaya Salad

    Ingredients
  • Salt
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 3 chilis
  • 1 tsp shrimp paste
  • 1 tsp grab paste
  • 4 tbsp Luang Prabang padaek
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp slated crab sauce
  • 15 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 whole lime
  • 3 cups shredded papaya
    Instructions
  1. Prepare the papaya: peel, wash, fuk, and set aside
  2. In a mortar and pestle, add a pinch of salt, garlic, and peppers then smash
  3. Add the shrimp paste and crab paste
  4. then mix together
  5. Add the padaek, fish sauce, tamarind, sugar, crab sauce and mix
  6. Add papaya to martar
  7. Slice tomatoes in half and gently smash into sauce and add lime and mix
  8. Mix and smash
  9. Enjoy!
